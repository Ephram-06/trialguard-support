Privacy Policy

TrialGuard Chrome Extension

Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TrialGuard ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

üîí Key Point: TrialGuard stores all your data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal subscription data to any external servers.

Information We Don't Collect

TrialGuard is designed with your privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Your subscription or trial information
  • Payment or financial data
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Location data

Local Data Storage

All subscription data you enter into TrialGuard is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Your data is tied to your Chrome profile. If you uninstall the extension, your data may be deleted depending on your browser settings.

Permissions We Use

TrialGuard requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage - To save your trials and subscriptions locally in your browser
  • alarms - To schedule reminder notifications before your trials expire
  • notifications - To display reminder alerts when trials are about to end

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension. No data is transmitted externally.

Notifications

TrialGuard may send you local notifications to remind you about upcoming trial expirations. These notifications are generated entirely within your browser and do not involve any data transmission.

You can enable or disable notifications in the extension settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

TrialGuard does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your privacy is our priority.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, it is protected by Chrome's built-in security features and your device's security measures (device password, encryption, etc.).

Data Export & Backup

TrialGuard provides features to export your data as CSV or JSON for backup purposes. These exports are saved directly to your device and are not uploaded anywhere.

Children's Privacy

TrialGuard does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The extension is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
